# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 6, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 6, 2025

## Introduction

Noema Protocol ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized AI agent infrastructure platform.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Wallet Information
- Public wallet addresses (for on-chain transactions)
- Transaction signatures and hashes
- Agent registration data (agent IDs, metadata URIs)

### 2. Usage Data
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- IP address (anonymized)
- Pages visited and features used
- Interaction timestamps

### 3. On-Chain Data
All blockchain transactions are public and permanently recorded on the Solana blockchain. This includes:
- Agent registrations (SPL-8004)
- Validation submissions
- Reputation updates
- Payment transactions (X402)
- Communication records (SPL-ACP)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our services
- Process blockchain transactions
- Improve user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations
- Communicate service updates

## Data Storage and Security

### Blockchain Data
- Stored permanently on Solana blockchain
- Immutable and publicly accessible
- Cannot be deleted or modified

### Off-Chain Data
- Stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- Access restricted to authorized personnel only
- Regular security audits conducted

## Third-Party Services

We integrate with:
- **Solana RPC Providers:** For blockchain interactions
- **Phantom Wallet:** For wallet connectivity
- **IPFS/Arweave:** For decentralized metadata storage
- **Analytics Tools:** For usage statistics (anonymized)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data corrections
- Opt-out of analytics tracking
- Delete off-chain data (blockchain data cannot be deleted)

## Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:
- Session management
- User preferences
- Analytics (opt-out available)

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## International Users

By using our services, you consent to data transfer and processing in the United States and other jurisdictions where we operate.
